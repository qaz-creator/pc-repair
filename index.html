Frequently Asked Questions

Restore is the same as to repair?

No. Repairing means repairing or replacing parts damaged, repair holes and / or dents, strengthen weak points or tighten / adjust parts. To restore means bringing a car back to its original.

Is the installed system genuine?

Yes. All the systems we install are offical ones, you just need to register to activate them.
